Suggestions for the Manual Collections Page

I set up a Shopify store to test these instructions. I found that they are clear and easy to follow; however, there are some suggestions that I would like to make in three areas: document structure, document consistency, and grammar. I will address each of these areas in the following sections.

Document Structure

The What to do next section does not fit with the organizational scheme of the rest of the page. Each of the other section titles begins with an imperative verb (create, add, and remove) and a clear description of the task involved in each section. What to do next does not provide this clear description. The contents do not seem to warrant a section of their own because they are not a complete piece of information. A more accurate title might be Adding a link to your manual collection from your website navigation; however, you have to navigate to another page to find that information. Also, it does not seem likely that someone will need to navigate to this section before completing the first, Create a manual collection. My suggestion is to incorporate this paragraph into the first section as it does not seem very useful to create a collection if customers cannot find it.
